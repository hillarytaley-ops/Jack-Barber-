NEON DATABASE SETUP (free) — Jack's Barber Style
=================================================

One-time setup so bookings on your live website appear automatically in
Staff area → Client Bookings.

YOU ARE USING: Neon (Serverless Postgres) — free plan is enough.

STEP BY STEP
------------
1. Open https://vercel.com/dashboard

2. Open your project: **jack-barber**
   (same project that hosts your live website)

3. Click the **Storage** tab at the top

4. Find **Neon** in the list (green logo, "Serverless Postgres")

5. Click **Create** on the Neon row

6. When asked for a plan, choose **Free** ($0/month)

7. Name the database something like: **jacks-barber-db**

8. Click **Create** / continue until you see **Connect to Project**

9. Select project **jack-barber**
   - Enable **Production** (and Preview if you want)
   - Click **Connect**

10. Go to **Deployments** tab → latest deployment → **⋯** menu → **Redeploy**
    (Required — the site must redeploy to pick up the database)

TEST
----
1. Open your live website
2. Submit a test booking (must pick a **service** from the dropdown)
3. You should see a green **Request sent** message
4. Open **Staff area** → sign in (admin / JackStyle2026)
5. Click **Client Bookings** — your test booking should appear

LOCAL PC (no Neon needed)
-------------------------
Double-click **START-SERVER.bat** → http://localhost:3000/admin/

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
- Bookings still missing? Make sure you **Redeployed** after connecting Neon.
- Still empty? Wait 1–2 minutes after redeploy, hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R).
- Change staff password after first login: Staff area → Security.

You do NOT need Supabase for this website.
