MAKE LIVE BOOKINGS SHOW IN STAFF AREA (one-time, ~5 minutes)
============================================================

Bookings need a small online database. Render.com hosts this free for you.

Steps:
1. Go to https://dashboard.render.com/
2. Click **New +** → **Blueprint**
3. Connect GitHub → choose repo **Jack-Barber-**
4. Render reads `render.yaml` automatically
5. Click **Apply** and wait until the service shows **Live**
   (URL: https://jacks-barber-style.onrender.com)
6. In Vercel, redeploy the website (Deployments → Redeploy)

After that:
- Customer books on your website → saved automatically
- You open Staff area → Client Bookings → booking appears

Local PC testing still uses START-SERVER.bat (no Render needed).

Staff login on live site: admin / JackStyle2026 (change in Security after first login)
