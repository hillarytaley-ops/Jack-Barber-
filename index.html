Our Roots

In many African communities, the barbershop is more than a place to get a haircut — it's a gathering space, a storyteller's corner, and a seat where identity is shaped with every pass of the clippers.

At Jack's Barber Style in Wodonga, we carry that spirit. Whether you're after a crisp skin fade, a perfectly shaped afro, twists, or a custom hair design, our barbers work with your natural texture — not against it.

"Your hair tells a story. We help you write the next chapter."

From Chair to Crown

Our Process

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Tell us your vision — bring photos or references. We assess your hair type and recommend the best approach for your texture and lifestyle.

  2. 02

    Prep & Treat

    Clean, detangled hair makes all the difference. We may apply a hot towel or moisturising prep to soften and protect your scalp.

  3. 03

    The Cut

    Clippers, scissors, and comb work in harmony. Whether shaping an afro or blending a fade, every pass is deliberate.

  4. 04

    Finish & Style

    Line-up, edge work, and styling products suited for afro-textured hair. You leave fresh, shaped, and ready.

Note: If we recommend a different service during consultation, pricing may adjust — so you always get the right cut for your hair.

Questions

FAQs

Do you specialise in different afro styles?

Yes — from traditional afro shapes to modern fades, tapers, twists, and custom designs. We work with your natural texture every time.

Can I get a fade with my afro cut?

Absolutely. Combining an afro shape with a skin or taper fade is one of our most popular styles.

How should I prepare my hair before the cut?

Arrive with clean, detangled hair. If you're unsure, call ahead — we're happy to guide you.

What maintenance do you recommend after an afro cut?

Regular moisturising and periodic shaping keep your afro healthy and defined. We can recommend products suited to your hair.

Do you walk in, or is booking required?

Walk-ins are welcome when chairs are free, but booking guarantees your spot — especially on weekends.
