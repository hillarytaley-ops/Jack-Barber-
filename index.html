Questions

FAQs

Do you specialise in different afro styles?

Yes — from traditional afro shapes to modern fades, tapers, twists, and custom designs.

Can I get a fade with my afro cut?

Absolutely. Combining an afro shape with a skin or taper fade is one of our most popular styles.

How should I prepare my hair before the cut?

Arrive with clean, detangled hair. Call ahead if you're unsure — we're happy to guide you.

Do you walk in, or is booking required?

Walk-ins welcome when chairs are free, but booking guarantees your spot — especially on weekends.
