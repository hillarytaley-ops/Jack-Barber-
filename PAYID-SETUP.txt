PAYID PAYMENTS — Jack's Barber Style
=====================================

Clients pay by PayID (instant bank transfer from their banking app).
No card processors. No Stripe.

STEP 1 — STAFF LOGIN PASSWORD (required on live site)
-----------------------------------------------------
1. In Vercel → jack-barber → Environment Variables, add:
   ADMIN_PASSWORD = a strong password you choose (min 8 characters)
2. Redeploy
3. Sign in at /admin with username: admin and your ADMIN_PASSWORD
4. Change password again in Staff → Security (recommended)

STEP 2 — REGISTER YOUR PAYID WITH YOUR BANK
-------------------------------------------
1. Open your business bank app or internet banking
2. Go to PayID settings and create a PayID using one of:
   - Mobile: 0478268399
   - Email: Jacqueskatumbulu@gmail.com
   - ABN (if registered for GST)
3. Link it to your business transaction account
4. Note the exact business name shown to payers (must match what customers see)

STEP 3 — ADD SETTINGS TO VERCEL
-------------------------------
1. Open https://vercel.com/dashboard → project **jack-barber**
2. **Settings** → **Environment Variables**
3. Add:

   PAYID = 0478268399
   (or your email PayID, e.g. Jacqueskatumbulu@gmail.com)

   PAYID_NAME = Jack's Barber Style
   SITE_URL = https://jack-barber-rho.vercel.app

   Optional — GST on tax invoices and booking receipts:
   GST_REGISTERED = true
   BUSINESS_ABN = XX XXX XXX XXX

   Optional — tax invoice emails when you click Mark paid in Staff:
   RESEND_API_KEY = (from resend.com — see REMINDERS-SETUP.txt)

4. Redeploy after saving.

OPTIONAL — AZUPAY (AUTOMATIC PAYID CONFIRMATION)
------------------------------------------------
For automatic booking confirmation when payment arrives (no manual check):

1. Sign up at https://www.azupay.com.au
2. Get API credentials from the Azupay dashboard
3. Add to Vercel:

   AZUPAY_CLIENT_ID = ...
   AZUPAY_SECRET = ...
   AZUPAY_API_URL = https://api.azupay.com.au

4. Set webhook URL in Azupay dashboard:
   https://jack-barber-rho.vercel.app/api/payments/webhook

5. Redeploy

If Azupay is not configured, clients still pay via your static PayID and you
confirm payment in Staff → Bookings when funds appear in your bank.

HOW IT WORKS
------------
1. Client fills in the booking form
2. They see PayID payment instructions:
   - PayID address
   - Exact amount (from your Services list)
   - Booking reference (e.g. b1719567890123)
   - GST breakdown (if GST_REGISTERED=true)
3. Client pays from their banking app using PayID
4. Click **Mark paid** in Staff → Client Bookings (records payment + emails tax invoice if RESEND is set)
5. Set booking status to **Confirmed** for reminders

GST & TAX INVOICES
------------------
- Barber services are taxable supplies in Australia
- Register for GST if turnover is $75,000+ (or voluntarily)
- Website prices are GST-inclusive when GST_REGISTERED=true
- GST amount = total price ÷ 11
- Tax invoices email automatically when you click **Mark paid** (needs RESEND_API_KEY)
- See docs/PayID-GST-Integration-Guide-Jacks-Barber-Style.pdf for full details

LOCAL PC
--------
Without PAYID set, bookings work as requests only (no PayID instructions shown).
Staff can still record payments manually in the admin area.

REMOVING STRIPE
---------------
This project no longer uses Stripe. Remove any old environment variables:
  STRIPE_SECRET_KEY
