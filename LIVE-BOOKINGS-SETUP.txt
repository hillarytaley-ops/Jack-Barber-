ENABLE AUTOMATIC BOOKINGS → STAFF AREA (one-time, ~2 minutes)
================================================================

After this, every booking on your website is saved and appears in
Staff area → Client Bookings automatically.

Steps in Vercel:
1. https://vercel.com/dashboard → open project **jack-barber-rho**
2. Click **Storage** tab
3. Click **Create Database** → choose **Postgres** (Neon)
4. Name it (e.g. jacks-barber-db) → Create
5. Click **Connect to Project** → select **jack-barber-rho** → Connect
6. Go to **Deployments** → **Redeploy** the latest deployment

That is all. No email app, no manual copy-paste of bookings.

Test:
- Submit a booking on the website (pick a service from the dropdown)
- Sign in to Staff area → Client Bookings → booking should appear

Local PC: use START-SERVER.bat — works without this step.

Alternative: deploy render.yaml on Render.com (see RENDER-SETUP.txt).
