VERCEL STORAGE SETUP (required for online bookings + staff dashboard)
======================================================================

Bookings and staff data must be saved in shared storage on Vercel.
Without this, bookings look "sent" but do not appear in the staff area.

Steps (one-time, ~2 minutes):
1. Go to https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Open your project (jack-barber-rho)
3. Click the "Storage" tab
4. Click "Create Database" → choose "KV"
5. Name it (e.g. jacks-barber-kv) and create it
6. Click "Connect to Project" → select this project → Connect
7. Redeploy the website (Deployments → ... → Redeploy)

After that, bookings from the website will appear under Staff area → Client Bookings.

Local testing on your PC still uses START-SERVER.bat (no Vercel KV needed).
